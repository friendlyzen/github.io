What is Friendly Zen?

Friendly Zen is a relaxed and simple meditation practice. We are not trying to achieve anything or force ourselves to be different. We sit, we notice, and we allow ourselves to arrive as we are.

When the mind wanders, that is not a problem. It is part of the practice. When the mind is ready, it returns. Friendly Zen is about giving ourselves permission to sit gently, together or alone.

The practice is about...

  • Relaxed and simple meditation.
  • Not trying to achieve anything.
  • Welcoming and befriending all parts of ourselves.
  • Allowing the mind to wander and return naturally.
  • Making a comfortable space to sit.
  • Practising “just sitting”.
  • Sitting together in a friendly way.
