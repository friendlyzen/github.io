For Hard Core Folks: One Day Sits Coming Soon

Starting mid-August 2026

Time-table: 9am to 3pm. (Additional Early/late sits available for people staying overnight.) \\ Dharma talk / discussion by a teacher. Dokusan (one-on-one meeting with a teacher) available.

Venue: Linh Sơn Temple, 25 Banyan St., Bellbowrie

Transport Bus: Route 444, closest stop "Moggill Rd at Bellbowrie" on Timetable.

Accomodation available by donation.

Open Contact page for more information, to confirm attendance, or to inquire about accomodation

Those with disabilities or health limitations will be warmly catered to. Rest room with bed available.

Fee: $10 for the day for Abbott Sister Tri Hang to cover Temple costs. Dana: By donation for the teacher on the day.
