One Day Sits Coming Soon

Illness and Disability Friendly

Starting mid-August 2026

Time-table: 9am to 3pm. (Additional Early/Late sits available if staying overnight.)
Dharma talk / discussion by a teacher during the day. Dokusan (one-on-one meeting with a teacher) available.

What to Bring: lunch and meditation cushions. Cushions provided for people catching buses or who don't own any. Chairs also available.

Venue: Linh Sơn Temple, 25 Banyan St, Bellbowrie

Transport Bus: Route 444, closest stop "Moggill Rd at Bellbowrie" on Timetable.

Accomodation: If you'd like to stay at the temple for a night or two rooms are available by donation (only as you can afford).

Fee: $10 for the day for One Day Sit for Abbott Sister Tri Hang to cover Temple costs (only as you can afford).

Dana: By donation for the teacher of the day (only as you can afford).

Get in touch: via Contact Page for more information, to confirm attendance, or to inquire about accomodation.

Those with disabilities or health limitations welcomed and catered for.

Please get it touch to let us know what your needs are. We can make a timetable that suits you and is flexible. Private rooms are available for rest as required.
